S.N. Foley and J.L. Jacob. Specifying Security for Computer Supported Collaborative Working. Journal of Computer Security, Vol 3(4), 1994/5, pp233-254

Abstract CSCW systems provide computer support to facilitate cooperation between users. This paper proposes an approach to the formal specification of security requirements for CSCW applications, where a CSCW application is viewed as a collection of activities that users may participate in. The specification approach is straightforward, and can be used to capture a wide variety of security requirements, including integrity and confidentiality. It is illustrated with a case study of a secure electronic examinations system.
