Simon N Foley and Jeremy L Jacob

Specifying Security for CSCW Systems. Proceedings Computer Security Foundations Workshop , 1995, Kerry, Ireland. IEEE Press.

Abstract CSCW systems provide computer support to facilitate cooperation between users. In this paper we propose an approach for the formal specification of functionality requirements and confidentiality security requirements of a CSCW application. These requirements give rise to various safety and confidentiality properties that a CSCW system, supporting the application, should uphold. The specification technique is illustrated with a case study. \end{abstract}
